My Favorite Flowers

Tulip

Tulips are bright, beautiful flowers that bloom in spring and come in many colors.

  • Symbol of love and rebirth
  • Over 3000 varieties
  • Popular in gardens

Rosemary

Rosemary is a fragrant herb used in cooking, with small blue-purple flowers and evergreen leaves.

  • Symbol of memory
  • Used in food and medicine
  • Grows well in dry soil
