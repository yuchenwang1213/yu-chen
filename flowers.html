My Favorite Flowers

Tulip

Tulips are bright, beautiful, and come in many colors. They are one of the most popular spring flowers.

  • Symbol of love and rebirth
  • Popular in Netherlands
  • Come in over 3000 varieties

Rosemary

Rosemary is not only a beautiful plant with purple-blue flowers, but also a useful herb in cooking and medicine.

  • Symbol of remembrance
  • Fragrant and evergreen
  • Used in tea, cooking, and oil
