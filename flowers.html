My Favorite Flowers

Tulip（郁金香）

Tulips are bright, beautiful, and come in many colors. They are one of the most popular spring flowers.

  • Symbol of love and rebirth
  • Popular in Netherlands
  • Come in over 3000 varieties

Rosemary（迷迭香）

Rosemary is a fragrant herb with needle-like leaves. It is used in cooking and has a fresh, woodsy scent.

  • Used in Mediterranean cooking
  • Symbol of memory and loyalty
  • Can be grown in pots or gardens
